# Edgebric Trademark Policy

"Edgebric" and the Edgebric logo (the numbat mark) are trademarks of the Edgebric project.

## Permitted Uses

- Refer to the Edgebric project by name in articles, blog posts, talks, and documentation.
- State that your product or service integrates with, is built on, or is compatible with Edgebric (e.g., "Built with Edgebric," "Compatible with Edgebric").
- Use the name in community discussions, issue trackers, and pull requests.

## Prohibited Uses

- Use "Edgebric" or the numbat logo in a way that suggests your project, product, or service is officially endorsed by, affiliated with, or maintained by the Edgebric project — unless you have written permission.
- Use "Edgebric" as part of your own product or company name (e.g., "Edgebric Cloud," "Edgebric Pro").
- Modify the Edgebric logo and present the modified version as official.
- Sell merchandise bearing the Edgebric name or logo without written permission.

## Forks

If you fork Edgebric, you must rename your fork and remove the Edgebric logo. You may state that your project is "based on Edgebric" or "a fork of Edgebric."

## Questions

If you're unsure whether your use is permitted, open an issue or email the maintainer.
