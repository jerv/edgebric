# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## Welcome

Welcome to Acme Corp. This handbook outlines our policies, benefits, and expectations.
All employees are expected to read and comply with these policies.

## Time Off

### Vacation

Full-time employees accrue vacation time based on years of service:

- 0–1 year: 10 days per year
- 1–3 years: 15 days per year
- 3+ years: 20 days per year

Part-time employees accrue vacation on a pro-rated basis.
Unused vacation up to 5 days may be rolled over to the next calendar year.
Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.

### Sick Leave

All employees receive 10 sick days per year. Unused sick days do not roll over.
A doctor's note is required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.

### Holidays

Acme Corp observes the following 11 paid holidays per year:
New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Presidents Day, Memorial Day,
Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day, Columbus Day, Veterans Day,
Thanksgiving, and Christmas Day.

## Health Benefits

### Medical Insurance

Acme Corp offers medical coverage through BlueCross BlueShield.
The company covers 80% of the premium for employee-only coverage and
60% of the premium for family coverage. Enrollment is available during
the annual open enrollment period or within 30 days of a qualifying life event.

### Dental and Vision

Employees may elect dental and vision coverage. The company contributes $50/month
toward dental premiums and $20/month toward vision premiums.

### 401(k) Retirement Plan

Employees are eligible to participate in the 401(k) plan after 90 days of employment.
Acme Corp matches 100% of the first 3% of employee contributions, and 50% of the
next 2%. The company match vests over a 3-year graded schedule:
33% after year 1, 66% after year 2, 100% after year 3.

## Code of Conduct

### Workplace Behavior

Acme Corp is committed to a respectful workplace. Harassment, discrimination,
and retaliation of any kind are strictly prohibited. Employees who witness or
experience misconduct should report it to HR or through the anonymous hotline.

### Conflicts of Interest

Employees must disclose any outside employment or financial interests that may
conflict with their duties at Acme Corp. Secondary employment that competes
directly with the company is not permitted.

### Confidentiality

Employees are required to protect the company's confidential information,
including trade secrets, customer data, and business strategies. This obligation
continues after the employment relationship ends.

## Remote Work Policy

Full-time employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
Employees must be reachable and available during core hours: 10am–3pm local time.
Home office equipment stipends of up to $500 are available upon manager approval.

## Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted annually in December. Mid-year check-ins occur in June.
Merit increases are effective January 1 of the following year and are based on performance
rating, budget availability, and market benchmarks.

## Termination

### Voluntary Resignation

Employees are requested to provide at least 2 weeks' written notice.
Final pay will be issued on the next regular payday following the last day of work.

### Involuntary Termination

Employees terminated without cause will receive severance pay equal to 2 weeks
of base salary for each full year of service, up to a maximum of 26 weeks.
Employees terminated for cause are not eligible for severance.
