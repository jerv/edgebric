# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## 1. Company Overview

Acme Corp was founded in 2019 by Sarah Chen and Marcus Williams. The company is headquartered in Austin, Texas with satellite offices in Denver and Portland. As of 2025, the company employs approximately 340 people.

## 2. Benefits

### 2.1 Health Insurance

All full-time employees are eligible for health insurance starting on their first day. Acme Corp offers three plans:

- **Gold Plan**: $150/month employee contribution, $500 deductible, 90% coinsurance
- **Silver Plan**: $80/month employee contribution, $1,500 deductible, 80% coinsurance
- **Bronze Plan**: $0/month employee contribution, $3,000 deductible, 70% coinsurance

Dental and vision coverage is included in all plans at no additional cost. The annual out-of-pocket maximum is $6,000 for individual coverage and $12,000 for family coverage.

### 2.2 Paid Time Off

Full-time employees receive PTO based on years of service:

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Days |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| 0-2 years       | 15 days         |
| 3-5 years       | 20 days         |
| 6-10 years      | 25 days         |
| 10+ years       | 30 days         |

PTO does not roll over. Unused PTO is forfeited on December 31st of each year. Employees may not take more than 10 consecutive PTO days without VP-level approval.

### 2.3 Parental Leave

Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers receive 8 weeks of fully paid leave. Parental leave must be taken within 12 months of the birth or adoption.

### 2.4 401(k) Retirement Plan

Acme Corp matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of salary, and 50% of the next 2% of salary. The maximum employer match is therefore 5% of salary. Vesting is immediate — there is no vesting schedule.

## 3. Work Policies

### 3.1 Remote Work

Acme Corp operates on a hybrid model. Employees are expected in-office Tuesday through Thursday. Monday and Friday are remote-flexible. Fully remote exceptions require director-level approval and are reviewed quarterly.

### 3.2 Working Hours

Core working hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time. Employees are expected to work approximately 40 hours per week. Overtime for non-exempt employees is paid at 1.5x the regular hourly rate.

### 3.3 Expense Policy

Business expenses over $500 require pre-approval from a manager. Receipts must be submitted within 30 days. Reimbursement is processed within 2 pay periods (approximately 4 weeks). The company credit card may be used for expenses up to $2,000 without pre-approval.

## 4. Performance & Growth

### 4.1 Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted semi-annually in June and December. Reviews follow a 5-point scale: Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Developing, Needs Improvement, Unsatisfactory.

### 4.2 Promotion Criteria

Promotions require: (a) at least 12 months in current role, (b) two consecutive "Meets Expectations" or higher reviews, and (c) manager recommendation with skip-level approval.

## 5. IT & Security

### 5.1 Password Policy

Passwords must be at least 14 characters and include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. Passwords expire every 90 days. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all systems.

### 5.2 Data Classification

Data is classified into four tiers: Public, Internal, Confidential, and Restricted. Customer PII is always classified as Confidential or higher. Restricted data requires encryption at rest and in transit.

### 5.3 Incident Response

Security incidents must be reported to security@acmecorp.com within 1 hour of discovery. The incident response team will triage within 4 hours. Critical incidents trigger an all-hands bridge call.
