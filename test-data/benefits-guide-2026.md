# Acme Corp Benefits Guide 2026

**Open Enrollment Period:** November 1 - November 30, 2025
**Coverage Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## 1. Medical Insurance

### 1.1 Plan Options

| Plan | Monthly Premium (Employee) | Monthly Premium (Family) | Deductible | Out-of-Pocket Max |
|------|---------------------------|-------------------------|------------|-------------------|
| **PPO Gold** | $125 | $425 | $500 / $1,000 | $3,000 / $6,000 |
| **PPO Silver** | $75 | $275 | $1,500 / $3,000 | $5,000 / $10,000 |
| **HDHP Bronze** | $45 | $175 | $3,000 / $6,000 | $7,050 / $14,100 |

*Acme subsidizes 80% of employee-only premiums and 70% of family premiums. Amounts shown are the employee's share after subsidy.*

### 1.2 Health Savings Account (HSA)
Available with the HDHP Bronze plan only:
- **Acme contribution**: $750/year (individual) or $1,500/year (family), deposited quarterly
- **Employee contribution limit**: $4,300 (individual) or $8,550 (family) for 2026
- Funds roll over year to year and are portable if you leave Acme
- HSA debit card provided for qualified medical expenses

### 1.3 Flexible Spending Account (FSA)
Available with PPO Gold and PPO Silver plans:
- **Healthcare FSA limit**: $3,200/year
- **Dependent Care FSA limit**: $5,000/year
- $640 rollover permitted; remaining balance forfeited

### 1.4 In-Network Providers
Our plans use the Aetna network. To find in-network providers, visit aetna.com/find-a-doctor or call Member Services at 1-800-555-0188.

## 4. Life & Disability Insurance

### 4.1 Basic Life Insurance
Acme provides basic life insurance at 2x annual salary, up to $500,000 maximum. This coverage is fully paid by Acme and begins on your first day of employment.

### 4.2 Supplemental Life Insurance
Employees may purchase additional coverage up to 5x salary (max $1,000,000). Coverage up to 3x salary requires no medical exam if elected within 30 days of hire. Spouse and dependent child coverage also available.

### 4.3 Short-Term Disability (STD)
- **Benefit**: 60% of base salary
- **Waiting period**: 7 days
- **Maximum duration**: 26 weeks
- Fully paid by Acme

### 4.4 Long-Term Disability (LTD)
- **Benefit**: 60% of base salary, up to $15,000/month
- **Waiting period**: 180 days (after STD exhausted)
- **Maximum duration**: To age 65
- Fully paid by Acme

## 5. Retirement Benefits

### 5.1 401(k) Plan
- **Eligibility**: After 90 days of employment
- **Acme match**: 100% of first 4% + 50% of next 2% = up to 5% match
- **Vesting**: 25% per year, fully vested after 4 years
- **2026 contribution limit**: $23,500 (under 50) / $31,000 (50+)
- **Investment options**: 25 index funds, 10 target-date funds, self-directed brokerage window
- **Roth 401(k)**: Available for after-tax contributions
- **Loan provision**: Up to 50% of vested balance, max $50,000

### 5.2 Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)
- **Discount**: 15% off market price
- **Contribution limit**: Up to 10% of base salary
- **Purchase periods**: Semi-annual (January 1 and July 1)
- **Lookback provision**: Price is based on the lower of the start or end of the purchase period

## 6. Wellness Programs

### 6.1 Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
Free, confidential counseling for employees and household members:
- Up to 8 sessions per issue per year
- Available 24/7 via phone: 1-800-555-0166
- Covers: mental health, substance use, financial counseling, legal consultation, relationship issues
- Sessions can be in-person, phone, or video

### 6.2 Wellness Stipend
$600/year wellness reimbursement for:
- Gym memberships or fitness classes
- Home fitness equipment
- Meditation or mindfulness apps
- Nutritional coaching
- Ergonomic equipment (standing desk, chair, etc.)

Submit receipts via the Benefits portal for reimbursement within 30 days.

### 6.3 Mental Health Days
In addition to PTO and sick leave, employees receive 2 designated Mental Health Days per year. These require no advance notice or explanation — simply mark "Mental Health Day" in the calendar.

## 7. Education & Development

### 7.1 Tuition Reimbursement
- Up to $5,250/year for job-related courses or degree programs
- Must receive grade of B or above (or Pass in P/F courses)
- Pre-approval required from manager and HR
- 2-year employment commitment after receiving reimbursement

### 7.2 Professional Development
- $1,500/year for conferences, certifications, and training
- Up to 3 paid days for conference attendance
- Company-sponsored LinkedIn Learning access for all employees

### 7.3 Internal Mobility
Employees who have been in their current role for 12+ months may apply for internal positions. Internal candidates receive priority consideration and a response within 10 business days.

## 8. Additional Perks

### 8.1 Commuter Benefits
- Pre-tax transit and parking contributions up to $315/month
- $50/month bike commuter reimbursement
- Free EV charging at all office locations

### 8.2 Cell Phone Reimbursement
- $75/month for employees required to be available by phone
- Requires manager approval; reviewed annually

### 8.3 Referral Bonus
- $5,000 for referred hires who complete 90 days
- $2,500 bonus for intern referrals
- No limit on number of referrals

### 8.4 Sabbatical
Employees with 7+ years of continuous service are eligible for a 4-week paid sabbatical. Must be taken within 12 months of eligibility. Subsequent sabbaticals available every 5 years.
