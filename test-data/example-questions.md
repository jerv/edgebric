# Example Questions for Testing

## Multi-Source Questions (pull from multiple data sources)

1. "What security measures should I follow when working remotely?"
   - HR Policies (remote work policy) + IT & Security (VPN, device security)

2. "What benefits do I get as a new employee starting next month?"
   - Benefits & Compensation (medical, dental, 401k) + HR Policies (onboarding, probation)

3. "I'm going on parental leave — what do I need to know about my benefits and time off?"
   - HR Policies (leave policy) + Benefits & Compensation (STD/LTD, FMLA)

4. "Can I use my personal laptop for work and get reimbursed?"
   - IT & Security (BYOD, approved devices) + Benefits & Compensation (cell phone/equipment reimbursement)

5. "What happens to my 401k and benefits if I leave the company?"
   - Benefits & Compensation (vesting, COBRA) + HR Policies (resignation/offboarding)

## Single-Source Questions

### HR Policies
- "How many vacation days do I get per year?"
- "What is the dress code policy?"
- "How does the performance review process work?"
- "Can I work remotely full-time?"

### Benefits & Compensation
- "What medical plans are available and how much do they cost?"
- "How does the 401k match work?"
- "What is the wellness stipend and what can I use it for?"
- "How do I enroll in the HSA?"

### IT & Security
- "What are the password requirements?"
- "How do I report a security incident?"
- "What software is approved for use?"
- "How do I connect to the VPN?"

## Topic Clustering Test (5 PTO-related questions)

Ask these 5 questions AND rate each response (thumbs up or down).
They should all cluster under a similar "PTO/vacation" topic in Analytics.

1. "How many vacation days do I get per year?"
2. "What is the PTO accrual policy?"
3. "Can I carry over unused vacation days to next year?"
4. "How do I request time off?"
5. "What happens to my PTO balance if I leave the company?"

**Important**: After asking each question, click the thumbs up or thumbs down
button on the response. Topics are only extracted when feedback is given.
Then check Analytics > Topic Trends — with min=1, even one rated query will show up.
